## Executive Summary

This intelligence briefing consolidates all active zoning cases, site plan extensions, and conditional use permit applications reviewed by the Austin Zoning & Platting Commission during the May–June 2026 session period. Each case entry includes the official case number, affected city district, project address, and a direct, immutable hyperlink to the City of Austin's official EDIMS staff evaluation document. This report is designed to give commercial developers, land acquisition teams, and zoning consultants a structured, verified feed of municipal activity — eliminating the need to manually parse fragmented city portal PDFs.

**Key Observations:** The June 2026 cycle shows concentrated activity in **District 5** (IH-35 corridor, south Austin) and **District 10** (central Austin), with multiple zoning change requests along the IH-35 expansion zone — a signal of potential large-scale commercial or mixed-use redevelopment. District 6 (northwest Austin) also shows new institutional and commercial zoning activity near Anderson Mill Road.
